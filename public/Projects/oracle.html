Oracle Projects

  • Bandai Spain


    I undertook a significant project at Bandai, a premier Japanese gaming and toy company. The project, based in France, necessitated the implementation of several Oracle EBS modules, including General Ledger (GL), Payables, Receivables, Cash Management, and Inventory.

    The project also required the replication of the French configuration in Spain, with adaptations to meet local requirements. This necessitated close collaboration with Bandai's French developers and IT staff. This collaboration was instrumental in designing the architecture of the desired solution and aligning it with the business and technical requirements of the Spanish localization. This was particularly true for aspects related to local accounting, invoice reporting (Suministro Inmediato de Información), Spanish logistics standards, and configurations for taxes and currency.

    A deep understanding of the business lifecycle and effective coordination with the French consultants were pivotal to the project's success. Equally important was the trust and rapport established with the key users of Bandai Spain.

    Throughout the project, I showcased exceptional problem-solving skills, diagnosing and resolving a range of issues from complex module configuration to challenges encountered during the User Acceptance Testing (UAT) phase.

    Communication was another area where I excelled. I effectively liaised with various stakeholders, including Bandai's business users, IT staff, and Oracle support. My communication skills were particularly noticeable during the training sessions I conducted for key users, ensuring they were well-equipped to use the new system.

    Project management skills were also integral to the project's success. I adeptly managed resources, met deadlines, and ensured the project adhered to its lifecycle. My ability to create clear and concise documentation proved invaluable in keeping the project on track.

    Throughout the project, I collaborated effectively with other consultants, developers, and business users. My commitment to continuous learning was also evident, as I stayed abreast of the latest Oracle updates and industry trends.

    In conclusion, my blend of technical knowledge, business acumen, and interpersonal skills, coupled with a commitment to continuous learning, played a crucial role in the successful implementation of Oracle EBS at Bandai.
